My Story: From Farm to AI

I didn't start in tech or had some lavish lifestyle growing up. I came from a working class — my dad worked for the town and my mom was a bank teller.

My first job was on a farm in Upstate New York picking corn and garlic, working long hours with my hands. From there, I transitioned into media, running a small content company. That move opened doors. Eventually, I found myself in Miami, working with some of the biggest names in the "make money online" industry, helping them implement AI into their content systems.

The Lightbulb Moment

My stepdad's boring tax consulting business

But the lightbulb moment came from something far less glamorous: my stepdad's boring tax consulting business.

Before moving to Miami, I used to help him with spreadsheets, data entry, and even cold LinkedIn outreach. Long, repetitive hours — the kind of work most people hate. Once AI started maturing, I realized I could replace those tedious tasks with workflows and agentic AI systems.

The result? I saved him the cost of a $50K/year marketing hire plus the $3K/month data entry specialist with benefits, while simultaneously bringing in more leads and revenue. That was nearly $100K in payroll saved every year — and more money in his pocket. More importantly, it gave my mom a better lifestyle.

Of course, I didn't charge him at first. But once he saw the impact, he paid me a hefty retainer to keep everything running. I even trained a VA to manage the workflows so I only had to check in a few hours a week.

That's when it clicked: If a boring, complicated tax business could win big with AI then every local and small business in America is sitting on this same opportunity.

And that's why I launched Grow AI to help others build scalable AI agencies that solve real business problems, charge retainers, and own a future-proof business in the process.
